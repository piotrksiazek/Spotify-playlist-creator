Getting started

Where is my spotify ID?

Spotify ID is an unique identifier linked to your personal account.
To find it you will need to:

  1. Click your username at the top right corner.
  2. Click under your profile.
  3. Hover mouse over share
  4. Click Copy Spotify URI
  5. Done! Now your Spotify ID is copied to your clipboard.

Where do created playlists go?

Playlists that are created via this website don't live on your personal Spotify account. They are hosted on [!link!] and to make them truly yours, you can subscribe to them.

How many playlists can I have?

Each user can create at most 3 playlists.
